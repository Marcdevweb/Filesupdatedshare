Ski Tech / Manger

Position: Ski Tech / Manager

We are searching for a dedicated and experienced Ski Tech/Manager who has a special expertise in Ski Racing and Retail.

Responsibilities:

  • Maintaining and tuning ski equipment to ensure optimal performance for our clients.
  • Overseeing retail operations, including inventory management and customer service.
  • Providing expert advice on ski racing equipment and techniques.
  • Managing and training junior staff to ensure top-notch service.
  • Handling customer queries, concerns, and feedback.

Qualifications:

  • Proven experience in ski tech roles, preferably in racing settings.
  • Strong knowledge of ski racing equipment and techniques.
  • Experience in retail management and customer service.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to multitask and manage time effectively.

Perks:

  • Icon Pass for next season or reimbursement at the end of the season.
  • Ski Days

If you're passionate about skiing, have the technical know-how, and can manage a fast-paced retail environment, we'd love to hear from you!
